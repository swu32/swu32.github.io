About

I am a PhD student in the Computational Principles of Intelligence Lab at the Max Planck Institute for Biological Cybernetics. During my PhD, I have been doing research on learning representations with a combination of computational modeling and cognitive experimental approaches. I have been applying the principles extracted from human learning to machines and use them to automate learning patterns from generic data. In the future, I hope this method can be used to solve general-domain scientific problems and social problems alike.
