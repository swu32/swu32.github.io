About

I am a Shanahan Foundation Fellow at the Allen Institute and the University of Washington. Prior to that, I conducted my PhD in Max Planck Institute for Biological Cybernetics, mentored by Eric Schulz, Peter Dayan, and Felix Wichmann. I received my master's degree at the Institute of Neuroinformatics in the University & ETH Zurich, and my bachelor's degree from the University of Rochester.
